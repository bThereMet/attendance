How it Works

Just follow there easy steps.

  1. The Instructor/ Attendance Taker creates a class account here.
  2. The attendees download the webapp and create accounts.
  3. The attendees take pictures in the app and the attendance taker verifies the students identities
  4. After this set up, attendance can be taken each meeting/class. The attendance taker just has to press a button, and the attendees scan the barcodes on the screeen and take a picture of themselves in the app.
  5. Attendance is taken.

The software uses facial recognition software to verify an attendee after the instructor does so the first time. The app verifies that the attendee is in the room by requiring them to first scan a changing barcode in the room. There is only enough time for them to scan a barcode and not enough time for the barcode to be sent to a friend before it is invalidated. No picture can be uploaded to the webapp and as a result, the student must be physically present at the location of the barcodes in order to take the picture in time. It's unbreakable.
